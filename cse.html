Why I like Computer Science

Before I was in college, I wanted to know how computers worked. I had no idea how a computer could open a document or a phone could send a text. Those are the kinds of things that really made me think. Now that I've been through a few years of college, I've realized that there is so much more going on than I could have imagined. That's what I love about computer science - it's everywhere. Not only is it everywhere, but it has totally changed the way our world operates. I can't think of any field that is as universal and promising as computer science is right now.

Android Deveolpment

Recently, I have been learning Java and Android development. One of other great things about computer science that I have come to appreciate is being a part of a community that encourages others to dive into new technologies. As a result, there are tons of resources available to help people learn and understand almost anything.
