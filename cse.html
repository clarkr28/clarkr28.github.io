Why I like Computer Science

Before I was in college, I wanted to know how computers worked. I had no idea how a computer could open a document or a phone could send a text. Those were the kinds of things that really made me think. The more computer science courses I have taken, the more I've realized that there is so much more going on than I could have imagined. That's what I love about computer science - it's everywhere. Not only is it everywhere, but it has totally changed the way our world works. I can't think of any other field that has had as much of an impact on our world as computer science. I can be a part of changing our world, and that's why I love computer science.

Android Deveolpment

Recently, I have been learning Java/Android development. One of the other great things about computer science that I have come to appreciate is being a part of a community that encourages others to dive into new technologies. If I don't know how to do sometthing, that just means I get to learn how to do something. I am extremely curious, and I love to learn. There are tons of resources at my fingertips to help me understand almost anything. Right now, that's Android development. I can't wait to find out what it will be tomorrow.
