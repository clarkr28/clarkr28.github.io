Experience

AK Steel

In the summer of 2016, I was a process control intern at AK Steel's Dearborn Work's plant. The process control department is responsible for exactly that - process controls. And when you have a fully integrated steel mill that produces 16,000,000 pounds of steel every day, you better believe there is a lot going on. While I was there I spent most of my time making a desktop application to store and maintain the necking data for AK's hot strip mill, PLTCM, and cold mill. I also made a few webpages on one of their internal websites that generated custom reports about material consumption during the steel making process.

Fraunhofer USA

Fraunhofer USA is a research company that has a branch located at MSU. Our location is Fraunhofer's Center for Coatings and Diamond Technologies (CCD). For my first few years there, I worked mostly on our machines for diamond research. I was responsible for maintaining and upgrading all of the software on these completely automated machines. Since the fall of 2016, I have transitioned to creating an android application for a new product we are developing. Although I worked at AK Steel in the summer of 2016, I have continued to work for Fraunhofer during my semesters at Michigan State.
