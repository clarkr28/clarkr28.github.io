About

My life at MSU

One word - busy. On top of taking computer science courses and everything else required by Michigan State, I'm on the MSU Drumline. That means I have 12.5 hours of rehearsal every week besides memorizing all the music on my own time. And don't forget about football games, which easily take up 10 hours of your Saturday. So I'm busy. Even though marching band makes my fall semesters a little hectic, I have countless memories that I wouldn't have made without it.

Personal Interests

Aside from being on the drumline, I really enjoy mountain biking on some of Michigan's great trails. I'm no pro, but I love to spend some time kicking up some dirt. Some of my best summer memories are riding cross country ski trails at some of Michigan's ski resorts.

I love the Red Wings, and I love hockey. As a matter of fact, I have a Red Wings game on right now. I don't mind watching almost any sport, but nothing keeps me on the edge of my seat like a good hockey game.

As much as I like hockey, there is a special place in my heart for Spartan Football. It's nearly impossible to be a member of the Spartan Marching Band and not have a burning passion for your team and your university.
