My life at MSU

For the past three years, I was on the MSU Drumline. Deciding to audition for the drumline was easily the best decision I have made in college. Even though I spent 12.5 hours a week in rehearsal and every Saturday was spent in marching band mode, I've made more memories and friends than I thought possible. This year I decided not to return to the drumline so I can enjoy my time on campus while I still have it. Even though I am no longer on the drumline, it still holds a special place to me. The memories I made were worth every minute of rehearsal and practice.

Now that I have more time on my hands, I decided to be a TA for our operating systems class. MSU is fortunate to have a great set of Computer Science faculty, and I have enjoyed working with them so far.

Personal Interests
